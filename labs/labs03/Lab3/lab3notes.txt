Blue flashes when the app opens/ as it enters the foreground (sceneWillEnterForeground)

Green stays once the app has fully opened across the screen (sceneDidBecomeActive)

Orange stays when you open the app in the background of the phone/open the multi-app viewer (sceneDidEnterBackground)

Brown flashes when the app fully closes on a single home button press (sceneWillResignActive)
